Writing

June 24, 2022

Denver University

During the fall semester of 2021, I applied to over a dozen law schools and was accepted to a little over half of them. Including but not limited to Suffolk University in Boston, Albany University in Albany, Hofstra University in Long Island, and Denver University in Denver Colorado.

After receiving my decisions I set out to visit many of the schools I applied to, in order to make a more informed decision before I committed to any one of these schools. Before I toured Denver, I took a roadtrip with my closest friends to see Albany University and Suffolk University.

My closest friends; Kara, Leslie, Emma and Diana

During our road trip we drove from Binghamton New York, to Albany New York, to Syracuse New York and then to Boston with a few stops. I drove both ways the entire time, the small blizzard we were driving through during the day of our departure didn't help my bad driving at all.

Me, on the steps of Albany Law School

During spring break of my senior year I traveled by myself to Colorado to tour Denver University. During my time there I was able to sit in on a class and was enthralled by how passionately the lecturer spoke. To be very honest the material was boring, and I was confused as I had no context for what they were learning, but the passion and care from the lectruer drew me in and sold me on the school.
